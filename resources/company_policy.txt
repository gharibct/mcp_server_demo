# Company Policy - Remote Work

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Our company embraces a flexible remote work model to promote a healthy work-life balance while ensuring operational excellence.

## 1. Remote Work Eligibility
- All full-time and part-time employees are eligible for remote work, provided their roles can be performed effectively outside the office.
- New hires may be required to work on-site during their initial onboarding period (typically 30 days).

## 2. Equipment and Setup
- The company will provide essential equipment, including laptops, monitors, and peripherals.
- Employees are responsible for ensuring a stable and secure high-speed internet connection.

## 3. Communication and Collaboration
- Use Slack for daily messaging and Zoom for video conferencing.
- Core working hours are from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM EST for synchronous collaboration.

## 4. Security
- Use the company-provided VPN for accessing all internal systems.
- Ensure your physical workspace is private and secure.
